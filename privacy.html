Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

At CV Crafted, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free resume builder service.

Information Collection and Storage

CV Crafted is designed with privacy in mind. We believe you should have complete control over your personal data.

Local Storage Only: All resume data you create (including your personal information, work experience, education, and skills) is stored locally in your browser's storage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We do not have access to your resume information
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services
  • Your information remains on your computer unless you choose to delete it

No Account Required

CV Crafted does not require you to create an account, which means:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • There are no passwords or login credentials to manage
  • You can use the service completely anonymously

Analytics and Cookies

We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use our website (such as page views and feature usage), but this data is:

  • Completely anonymous and cannot be tied to any individual
  • Used solely to improve the service
  • Not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes

Our website may use essential cookies for basic functionality. These are necessary for the site to work properly and cannot be disabled.

Third-Party Services

CV Crafted may use third-party services for hosting and analytics. These services may have their own privacy policies:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs) for faster page loading
  • Anonymous analytics services

We carefully select these services to ensure they respect user privacy and do not access your resume data.

PDF Export

When you export your resume as a PDF:

  • The PDF is generated entirely in your browser
  • No data is sent to our servers during this process
  • The file is created on your device and saved directly to your downloads folder

Data Security

Since your data is stored locally in your browser, security depends on:

  • Keeping your device secure with passwords and up-to-date software
  • Using a secure, updated web browser
  • Being cautious when using public or shared computers

Your Rights

Because we don't collect or store your personal data on our servers, you have complete control:

  • You can delete all your resume data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage
  • You can export and move your data by downloading your resume as a PDF
  • You can use the service without providing any personal information

Children's Privacy

CV Crafted is available to users of all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, there are no special considerations for minors. However, we encourage parents and guardians to be involved in their children's online activities.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how CV Crafted handles data, please contact us:

  • Through our contact form
  • By visiting the Contact page linked in the footer

In Summary: CV Crafted respects your privacy. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information. Your resume data stays on your device, and you have complete control over it. We're committed to providing a free, secure, and private resume building experience.
